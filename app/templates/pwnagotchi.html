Pwnagotchi Integration

Automate your handshake uploads directly from your Pwnagotchi to this server.

1. Install the Plugin

Log in to your Pwnagotchi via SSH and run this command to download the high-speed upload plugin:

wget https://raw.githubusercontent.com/EpicNori/hashcat-wpa-server/master/pwnagotchi/hwps.py -O /usr/local/share/pwnagotchi/installed-plugins/hwps.py
2. Configure your Settings

Edit your /etc/pwnagotchi/config.toml and add the following entry. Replace the IP with your server's IP address.

main.plugins.hwps.enabled = true
main.plugins.hwps.server_url = "http://{{ network_ip }}:9111/upload"
main.plugins.hwps.username = "admin"
main.plugins.hwps.password = "changeme"
main.plugins.hwps.auto_upload = true
3. How it Works

Once configured and restarted (systemctl restart pwnagotchi), your Pwnagotchi will:

  • Scan for new handshakes in the background.
  • Automatically upload them whenever it has an internet connection.
  • You can check the User Profile on this dashboard to see real-time updates!

Quick Tips

Bluetooth Tethering: Tether your phone to your Pwnagotchi to enable instant uploads while mobile.

Multiple Users: You can create separate operator accounts for each of your Pwnagotchi units.
